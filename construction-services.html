Custom Home Builders

(508) 566-1190

Collaborative and Seasoned Home Construction Contractors

At DC Builders, our seasoned custom home construction contractors collaborate with you throughout the pre-construction, construction, and post-construction process. We build beautiful homes throughout Falmouth, but we also service the cape and islands. It’s our goal to bring your dream home to life in an easy, stress-free manner. Since 1983, we’ve built homes on-time and within budget. 


During pre-construction, we listen to you throughout the process to understand your lifestyle and budget.


While your home is under construction, we conduct daily on-site construction meetings with our in-house crews. We work with vetted and trusted subcontractors, keeping everyone accountable throughout the entire build. From sequence to production and quality control, we stay focused on the goal. 


The last step in any build, post-construction includes site cleanup, systems training, final inspections, and move-in coordination. After completion, we have a final walkthrough to ensure your complete satisfaction.


Are you ready to get started? Contact us today for the home builders Cape Cod residents trust.

Our Contracting Services

Custom Homes

Our knowledgeable team works with architects, engineers, and designers to build energy-efficient custom homes that work for you and your family.

﻿

Additions and Remodeling

Home remodeling and addition projects don’t just make your home more comfortable and attractive, they also increase your home’s value significantly. Home improvements are an investment, and the contractor you choose can impact the return on your investment.﻿

Kitchens and Baths

Kitchen and bath remodeling is probably the single best home improvement you can make to increase the value of your property without adding square footage. The rate of return on a kitchen and/or bathroom remodel is so high that the project can easily pay for itself.

﻿

Roofing and Siding

We have you covered when it comes to roofing and siding work with installation, patching, and strip-and-replace services.

Decks

We’re experts at designing and installing custom decks or restoring existing ones. We consider the architecture of your home for a cohesive deck installation that complements your property. 

Painting

Pick a color, any color – pick as many as you like. Our experienced painters take great pride in being the icing on the cake of your home improvement project! 

Moisture Repairs

We can replace all exterior trim or a single piece of compromised material with a maintenance-free composite. We have the time, talent, and tools to do the job.

Miscellaneous Repairs

From replacing doors, windows, and skylights to finally swapping out your old shag carpeting for the floor covering of your choice, we can tackle any repair challenge.
