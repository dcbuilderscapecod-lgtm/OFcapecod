Website Accessibility Policy

DC Builders is committed to ensuring that our website is accessible to everyone, including people with disabilities. We strive to meet or exceed the requirements of the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1, Level AA.

Measures we take

We work to make our site perceivable, operable, understandable, and robust: descriptive alternative text on images, sufficient color contrast, keyboard-navigable menus and forms, clear headings and labels, and content that works with screen readers and other assistive technologies.

Ongoing effort

Accessibility is an ongoing effort. We regularly review our website and address issues as we find them. Some third-party content or features may not yet fully conform, and we are working to improve them.
